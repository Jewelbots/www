What are Jewelbots?

Jewelbots are programmable friendship bracelets for teens and pre-teens. Right out of the box, girls can easily program the bracelet to light up when their friends is near or send messages to a buddy across the classroom. These are not just pretty jewelry, they are a powerful communication tool.

How Does it work

Jewelbots use bluetooth to communicate with each other, and can be used with or without a phone. Girls can sync their Jewelbots with their friends, or program them with the Jewelbots app. Using basic if/then statements, girls begin learn the fundamentals of computer science while engaging with their friends and expressing themselves.

No boundaries, Open source

For those girls who want to continue to build upon their programming skills, they can use the Arduino IDE, and make the bracelets do whatever they can imagine. Jewelbots are open source, so for the curious tinkerer, they offer the perfect platform to explore what is possible with wearable technology communication. Light up when they get an Instagram follower, or when they get a text from mom, or their favorite show is about to start.

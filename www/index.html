What are Jewliebots?

Jewliebots are programmable friendship bracelets aimed at teenage girls. Girls can make the bracelet light up when their friends is near or send messages to a buddy across the classroom.
Jewliebots are more than just pretty jewelry, they are a powerful communication tool.

How Does it work

Jewliebots are a mesh network of jewelry that can be programmed using a simple app. Using basic if/then statements, girls begin learn the language of computer science while engaging with their friends and expressing themselves. (having fun?)

No boundaries, Open source

Jewliebots are also open source, so for the curious tinkerer, they offer the perfect platform to explore what is possible with wearable technology communication. Using the Arduino IDE, girls can literally make the bracelets do whatever they dream up. Light up when they get an Instagram like, why not? Other example?
